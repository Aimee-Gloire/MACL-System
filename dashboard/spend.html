Budget & Spend

Console chevron_right Spend Approval
info

No money moves on-chain. MACL only records a budget and approves spend requests against it (2-of-3). A spend request attaches a supporting document (a quotation, proforma invoice, purchase order or cost breakdown) that justifies the spend — not a receipt, as the money has not been spent yet. The file stays on your machine; only its SHA-256 fingerprint is stored, so anyone can later prove the document has not been altered or swapped after approval.

Set programme budget

Donor-Admin only. A budget can be set or changed while the agreement is still a draft; it locks once the agreement is finalised.

Raise a spend request

NGO only. Pick a finalised, budgeted agreement, then attach a supporting document — its fingerprint is computed in your browser and the file never leaves your machine.
